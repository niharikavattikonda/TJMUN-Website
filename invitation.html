TechMUN 2016 Invitation



Dear Esteemed Sponsors and Club Leaders,


My name is Will Frank and I am a senior at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology.

As the Secretary-General for the Model United Nations club at TJHSST and the Secretary-General of TechMUN 2016, it is my distinct honor and pleasure to invite you to TechMUN 2016, will be held on April 15-16, 2016 at Glasgow Middle School.

At this time, I am excited to announce that registration is open! Please visit our website here to register your delegates. You can choose which committees your delegates are most interested in and register accordingly.

Information about committees and topics (including pertinent logistics regarding application-only committees) is on the website, and background guides will be posted in the coming weeks.

As we near the conference, detailed information about conference rules and delegate notifications will also be posted on the website.

TJMUN prides itself on our dedication to excellence, and we hope those of you who attended TechMUN in previous years will be able to attest to its merit. This year, we have a variety of innovative committees planned, striving towards our goal of creating a unique delegate experience, and enabling every delegate to make the most of their two days debating world issues.

Please feel free to contact us with any questions or concerns at tjmodelun@gmail.com. Our sponsor, Ms. Alexandra Pou, can be reached at aypou@fcps.edu.

Thank you for your patience and cooperation, and we sincerely hope to see you in April!
Will Frank

Secretary-General | TJMUN 2015-2016 and TechMUN 2016
